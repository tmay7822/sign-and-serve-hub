### Objection Handling

**"The price seems high"**
- "Let me break this down: you're getting 25-75 professionally written pages. That's $33-53 per page. A basic one-page website costs $2,000+ and doesn't get you local SEO rankings."

**"I don't need that many location pages"**
- "Here's the thing - your competitors are showing up for '[City] notary' searches and you're not. Each page targets customers in that specific area. It's like having 25-75 salespeople working 24/7."

**"I can build my own website"**
- "Absolutely, and you could also do your own legal work. But your time is worth $150+/hour doing notary services, not learning web development. We'll have you live and ranking in 48 hours."

**"I need to think about it"**
- "I understand this is an investment. Let me ask - how much business do you estimate you lose each week from not having proper local SEO? If it's even 2-3 signings per month, this pays for itself in 90 days."
